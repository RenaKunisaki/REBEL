Rena's Extra Blocks & Extended Logic: adds a bunch of nifty new blocks.

Physics Blocks
- Bounce Blocks: repels things away on touch.
- Boost Blocks: accelerates things in a specific direction on touch. Right-click to change direction since hammer doesn't work on non-solid tiles.

Cheaty/Fun Blocks
- Heal/Hurt Blocks: Heals or hurts anyone who touches them.
